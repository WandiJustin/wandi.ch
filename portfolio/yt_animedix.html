YouTube - Animedix

History

The channel "Animedix" was one of the longest projects I was working on. I've stopped working on this channel in early 2019. The start of this channel was on the 22.11.2014. I was heavily into AMVs and Video game Edits at the time and wanted to create such videos myself which I made.
I've worked with various games, programs, plugins, presets and series.

How to create a video

I tried to create a more visual appealing look. I was way more focused on filters and effects. Most parts of the video were from game trailers combined with footage from me while I was playing or footage from various series.
I used recording software to record my footage. My main tool is the Open Broadcaster Software, in which I will collect the game session. I first started using OBS with this channel and worked with it since and used various plugins.
After collecting the footage, trailers and or series I will edit those together to a series of clips in Premiere Pro. In Premiere Pro I try to match the clips to my selected music. I will export that into After Effects and use various motion FX and filter plugins to make the visuals more appealing.


Apperance

I didn't really have a theme for this channel. I worked closely with a close friend of mine Koezan for most of my decisions on appearance. He influenced this channel heavily. A theme that was almost there from the start was a (broken) heart. It's in the beginning and end of a video, and also in the channel avatar and banner.
