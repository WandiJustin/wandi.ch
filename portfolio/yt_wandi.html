YouTube - Wandi

History

"Wandi" is my newest YouTube Channel and the only one that I'm still activly working on. I've created the channel on the 18.03.2016. But started really working on it in 2019. I've uploaded my first video on the channel in 2019. I've played the game Left 4 Dead with Koezan (Samuel) and Flowey. After that video I've uploaded more and more videos. Until recently, since then I'm trying to upload atleast one video a month.

How to create a video

The point of editing the video is to cut together the funniest clips of me and my friends gaming and then putting a layer of text under it. The gameplay is recorded while I'm streaming on Twitch or just casually playing some video games.
I use 2 softwares to record my footage. My main tool is the Open Broadcaster Software in which I will collect the whole session. And the second tool is "Medal", with Medal I can click my mouse button and the last 5 minutes of my session will be recorded.
After collecting the footage I will edit those sessions or clips of the session together in Adobe Premiere Pro. Here most of the magic is done. I use the normal text-tool of Premiere Pro and use the Font "Keep Calm" with an border. All of the moving text is made with frame-for-frame set positions (keyframes). I export the endproduct in H.264 and upload that onto my channel.


Apperance

The apperance of the youtube channel itself was mostly made with Photoshop. I used photoshop to cut the profil picture, watermark and other pictures to the right size. The banner was also made with basic photoshop and wasn't that big of a deal. I went for a rather clean look than something to overloaded. I think the videos are already to much chaos so why should the channel be that. But I still love the Vaporwave-Style and so I wanted to integrate that into the banner. I don't do thumbnails. I take the best one that youtube gives me to choose from when the video is finished uploading.
